Useful links

National Tax and Customs Administration

Here you can find the latest news about doing taxes, help for understanding the contents and methods of declarations, databases about taxpayers, gas prices and consumption norms, calculator for determining late payment surcharge or self-audit allowance, and forms for requests, authorizations, declarations.

Online Companies' Register

Registered data of companies can be downloaded from here. It is possible to search based on company name, register number or tax number.

Central Office for Administrative and Electronic Public Services

Similar to the companies' register, but here you can query the data of private enterprises. Aside from that, a few forms are available here, which enterpreneurs need to submit at local government offices.

Government Portal

Here can you send in your electronic declaration, check you tax account and tax-related information. The Tax Administration sends messages to you via this site. Aside from that, instructions can be found here for handling a number of official matters with the tax authority.
